Find Medicament

"Today, due to significant advancements in science, the variety of medications and diseases has increased."

"Therefore, as engineers, we came up with a solution proposal to ease the workload of our doctors, even if only slightly."

"This project is planned to reach patients' health in a more accurate and less overlooked manner, while also aiming to minimize the waste of valuable time for doctors and patients."





"Find Medicament" is a project where, doctors no longer need to spend a long time examining each medication to determine which symptoms it alleviates or for which disease it is intended to treat."

"At the same time, by entering the name of the medication into this application, the correct treatment can be administered more quickly."
